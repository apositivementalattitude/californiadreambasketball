About

California Dream Basketball is a highly competitive youth and high school basketball program dedicated to providing our athletes with top notch instruction and training, to help them advance to the next phase of their basketball journey.

What We Offer

Our coaching staff has over 25 years of experience teaching the game and has produced several highly successfull basketball players over the years.

  • Play competitive basketball at the highest level.
  • Expert coaching focused o skill development and character building
  • Compete in regional and national basketball tournaments, gaining exposure to high-level competition and college scouting opportunities

We emphasize personal growth, discipline, and sportsmanship to help players excel in basketball and in life. We are proudly recognized as a leading youth travel basketball league in Southern California.

Why Join California Dream Basketball?

  • Train with experienced coaches who are passionate about developing young athletes.
  • Compete in elite regional and national tournaments.
  • Gain exposure to college scouts and advance your basketball journey.
